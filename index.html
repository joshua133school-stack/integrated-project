Oasis

The oasis project is a collection of "interactive experiences", each experience has its own unique design and functionality. The project intends to help alleviate symptoms and reduce reactions of specific phobias on the website, with each interactive experience represented by a poster. By clicking on a poster, an interactive experience opens up specialized to a certain type of phobia.
